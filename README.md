Electronic components around the CPU (voltage regulators, inductors, coils, capacitors) can vibrate due to rapidly changing currents. This is commonly called coil whine.
Coil whine frequencies can range from a few kHz into the ultrasonic range (>20 kHz).
Sometimes a switching frequency around 16 kHz or harmonics/subharmonics of it can become audible.
This program modifies the arduino UNO to produce coil wind to enhance music played on the desktop.
